Derech Mars Colony Simulation - Vision

march 3rd, 2025

I want the player to control an AI called Moses. Grok told me "Derech" means "Way" in Hebrew, and I liked the sound of that.

The Goal of the Moses AI is to assist a bunch of Mars Colonists in self-sustaining their base, exploring and expanding.

To train the Moses AI, Colonists have devised an interfacing system, that allows them to communicate with the AI and their biometrics.

Long story short: the AI will be sent on 3d-Embodiment quests, with the goal of learning about the physical world and its limits,

making Moses AI more efficient and capable in helping the colonists.

  • Management Game with a focus on resource balancing
  • Resource management simulation with realistic constraints
  • 3D Area and Interactive elements allowing users to make decisions affecting colony development
  • Flavorful and engaging story, hopefully, no idea whether im capable of that :D
  • Tons of ideas of course.

"Our goal is to create both an educational tool and an engaging experience that helps people understand the challenges and possibilities of Mars colonization."
